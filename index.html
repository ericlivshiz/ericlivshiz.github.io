About The Game

Viking Quest: The Green Crusade is a platforming RPG centered around the theme of sustainability.
Its goal is to provide an entertaining way to learn about pressing, real-world environmental issues.

Viking Quest combines gameplay mechanics, storytelling, and educational content to engage and inform players to take action

In the Near Future we hope to add:

- Many New levels with different themes that relate to real world issues

- Branching dialogue trees that help educate players on sustainability issues and create a fun game

- Links to real resources and advocacy groups where players can make a change in the real world

- Cross platform support, an example platform being IOS and ANDROID
